Donate

Your gift changes daily life

Meher Welfare Trust uses donations carefully: staples for families under stress, school supplies, emergency transport, and wages for field coordinators who know their communities.

Whether you give once or monthly, you help us plan ahead and respond when crises hit. Browse active campaigns to see what we are fundraising for right now, or give to our general fund and we will allocate where need is greatest.

Food & relief

Ration support, hot meals in emergencies, and nutrition awareness for vulnerable households.

Education & livelihoods

Learning materials, skills sessions, and small grants that protect dignity and build resilience.

Transparent use of funds

We aim for clear reporting so supporters see how every contribution supports real families.

For zakat, sadaqah, or questions about bank transfer and receipts, please contact us—we will guide you through the options.
