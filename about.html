About Us

Who we are

Meher Welfare Trust is a grassroots organisation built around one simple commitment: to stand with families who are overlooked or overwhelmed. We began with small local efforts and have grown into a network of volunteers, partners, and supporters in Pakistan and among the diaspora.

Our work spans food security, education support, emergency relief, and community-led programmes. We listen first, coordinate with local leaders, and aim for solutions that protect dignity—whether that is a ration pack delivered respectfully, a classroom supported with supplies, or signposting to health services.

Transparency matters to us. Supporters deserve to know how funds are used and to see impact beyond slogans. If you would like to collaborate or learn more, we would love to hear from you.
